Freaky Friday: Lindsay Lohan is live-tweeting the EU referendum results and it's amazing - but is it really her?

Save
  • Raziye Akkoc
24 June 2016 3:35am

Follow the author of this article

  • Raziye Akkoc

Follow the topics within this article

  • EU Referendum
  • European Union
  • Europe
  • Lindsay Lohan

Lindsay Lohan has become one of the highlights of a tense EU referendum results night as she offers herinsightful views on remaining in the EU, where Sunderland is and free trade.

Yes, free trade.

Her first tweet late on Thursday night was a video in which she appeared to advertise Chanel while showing her Instagram followers she was watching the BBC's EU referendum results show.

However there was some scepticism from some quarters that it was the real Lohan tweeting and the Telegraph has not been able to confirm it is her.

#besmart pay attention and work hard to buy @chanelofficial #remain where's Sunderland? Does Sarah Palin live there? Lol

A video posted by Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) on Jun 23, 2016 at 4:18pm PDT

One thing for sure the #referendum results are very close, but also showing a difference of opinion across #Britain

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

On Thursday, the UK went to the polls to vote on whether they should remain or leave the European Union.

During the course of a tense night, American Lohan provided commentary which had Twitter users hailing her analysis.

The Mean Girls star began by stressing that we were all people...

@bowiemeg #remain #weareallpeople #BeOne

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 23, 2016

...then she tweeted at the Independent newspaper's Twitter account blaming Sunderland for the drop in the pound

@Independent why? prove the UK will not trigger anger by allowing (sunderland) to lower the UK pound - THIS IMPACT WILL CONTROL ALL MARGINS

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 23, 2016

Then there was the odd tweet about the small shops with the hashtag, #Remain

#Remain small shops should pay more attention

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 23, 2016

And ensured she was polite by thanking the BBC for explaining to her where Sunderland and Swindon were...

@BBCBreaking @BBCWorld @BBCNews thank you for explaining where #Sunderland AND #Swindon ARE... #Remain #EUref

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 23, 2016

She tweeted at Bloomberg to show her love for peace, love and free trade (?!)

@markets #REMAIN #PEACE #ONELOVE #FREETRADE

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

Then got back to the questions one assumes a fair few are asking: where's Kettering (answer: Northamptonshire) while demanding to know why the BBC wasn't explaining what would happen if the UK left the EU.

#REMAIN Sorry, but #KETTERING where are you&why is this woman @BBCNews speaking on people rather than TELLING us what happens if UK LEAVES?

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

She loves the Shetlands...

i love the #shetlands @BBCNews thank goodness we have pure hearted people our side #besmart #ShetlandsVSSunderland #BringItOn

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

...but attacked Boris Johnson (wrongly, we add) for using his child's graduation during the campaign

@BorisJohnson using your child's graduation for CAMPAIGN? sounds like someone in USA who used a child out of wedlock for PR.... #Remain

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

@BorisJohnson #Trump what discussions are you having?

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

She said on her Twitter timeline she had not been hacked as the Telegraph engaged with her. However, there is no way to confirm it's not just a hacker saying this

@lindsaylohan You can find Kettering here https://t.co/t13ojNrghJ pic.twitter.com/SUDPdZxPEZ

— Telegraph TV & Radio (@TeleTVRadio) June 24, 2016

#remain #REMAIN @standardnews #remain #REMAIN @Telegraph unlike OBAMA's recent setback @BBCWorld i have not been hacked

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

i was referring to #sarahpalin #REMAIN #LoveIsLove

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

She even asked the Telegraph what Margaret Thatcher would think of a Brexit

@Telegraph what would #MARGARETTHATCHER THINK OF #Brexit ?

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

On Instagram she shared a video as she urged the UK not to let her down further revealing her true Remain colours

#remain #IAMIRISH #iloveuk #iloveEu #iloveusa #onelove #freetrade don't let me down in the UK as a loving member of your beautiful country during #hermajestythequeen 's birthday time

A video posted by Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) on Jun 23, 2016 at 5:46pm PDT

Then her despair increased as she pleaded with David Cameron and Sadiq Khan to say something

@David_Cameron #remain please what can i say to let people know how important it is to #stay @SadiqKhan @BBCNews @TheSun say something

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

Then questioned what was "wrong" with Manchester...

#bury what's wrong with you manchester??????

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

i lived in manchester!! be conscious! #fauxpaux @MaxGeorge @DannyCipriani87 #bury has left manchester WHY?

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

Although people pointed out Bury is not Manchester

Oi. Bury's not Manchester. https://t.co/8gBa2WAq2W

— Philip Collins (@PCollinsTimes) June 24, 2016

Her concern for the pound continued over the course of the night

good luck with the pound.. will take you about 15 years to get it back up #REMAIN instead

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

And if users didn't know already, she stressed she was against Brexit...

@DailyMailCeleb @DailyMailUK i am actually against BREXIT i am for #REMAIN so get your facts straight you lazy cow

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

@guardian yes.. as more research and care for all children will come if we #Remain

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

Then praised London for voting to Remain

@BBCNews well done #LONDON #remain is standing strong. America, do the same when you #VOTE in America in November @ft #besmart

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

She expressed concern over the economic impact...

dropping by the second... one dollar and forty nine cents.. dropping as we breathe..#REMAIN

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

While urging working class people to remain despite the polls closing

#Remain #besmart #onelone #lovenothate #FREETRADE

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

#WORKINGCLASSPEOPLE #REMAIN

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

She had some Twitter engagement with Lily Allen

@lilyallen that's my girl!

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

@lilyallen i am watching in bed

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

She continued to make Queen and Mean Girls references

for the love of #hermajestythequeen ..the #leave #youcantsitwithus FELLOWS @SNP WE SHOULD ALL BE SITTING TOGETHER #LOVENOTHATE we can be 1

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

She showed her love for Fife

thank you #Fife

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

She tried to rally those hopeful a last minute increase in Remain voters would happen

BE POSITIVE .. IGNORE THE 200,000 MARGIN #REMAIN

— Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) June 24, 2016

She linked a vote for Remain with compassion for refugee children

#Remain so that the UK, standing with the EU, HELP refugee children to have a brighter future #goalno8 #onelove #friends #freetrade #love

A photo posted by Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) on Jun 23, 2016 at 7:32pm PDT

Just after 4am, Lohan said she was off to bed and warned of the Brexit impact on the US elections...

This goes for the USA too #besmart I am getting extremely tired and off to sleep. With that being said, I am praying for the uk to #remain as it will have an effect on our next American president as well. Do your research.

A video posted by Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) on Jun 23, 2016 at 8:11pm PDT

As she reminded us to Remain with a picture of herself

#remain

A photo posted by Lindsay Lohan (@lindsaylohan) on Jun 23, 2016 at 8:15pm PDT

And it seems we're not the only ones who are enjoying her haranguing the BBC and other media organisations...

There is more referendum analysis going on on Lindsay Lohan's Twitter than I would have expected.

— Simon Underwood (@SiFoulaReel) June 23, 2016

Lindsay Lohan is angry at our economy. What is happening to Britain? https://t.co/HxZjMcQg2r

— Mark (@wilko2205) June 24, 2016

.@lindsaylohan tweeting on the EU Referendum. Next an article on @ConHome

— Lord Ashcroft (@LordAshcroft) June 24, 2016

Many wondered where she had been during the campaign and some urged her to become Labour's next leader (we think Jeremy Corbyn's position is safe... for now)

Where was Lyndsay Lohan during the campaign? It could have all been so different.

— Dr Heather Doran (@hapsci) June 24, 2016

Lyndsay Lohan for Labour Leader!!!!!!

— Cromerty York (@Cromerty) June 24, 2016
